Steiner Tree Game: Prove P=NP

Your goal is to connect all the dots. If you’re feeling resourceful, add new points to craft a shorter path—just click and drag to create a line.
The score at the bottom? That’s the computer’s best attempt, minus the lines you’ve used. Finish with a positive score, and you’ve quietly bested the machine. Quite satisfying, really.
Good luck out there girls!
